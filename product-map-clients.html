The Challenge

Ground crews run on informal tools.
That creates real gaps.

Your team works hard. But when coordination happens over group texts, printed checklists, and verbal handoffs, things fall through the cracks — especially when staff turns over.

📋
Tasks get missed
No shared checklist means no shared accountability. If it's not written somewhere everyone can see, it doesn't always get done.
🔄
Turnover breaks continuity
When a groomer leaves, their knowledge leaves too. New hires rely on whoever trained them — and that varies every time.
✈️
No visibility on inbound flights
Knowing when an aircraft is 30 minutes out changes how your team prepares. Without it, you're always reacting.
🚗
Vehicle status is always a guess
Fuel levels, mileage, service due dates — without a shared record, every shift starts with questions that should already have answers.
What Skycare Does

Everything your crew needs,
in one app on any phone.

No installation. No accounts to create. Open the link, install it to the home screen, and it works — online or offline.

✅
Shared task checklists
Every groomer sees the same daily and weekly task list, grouped by area. Progress is tracked in real time across all phones — when one person checks something off, everyone sees it.
📡
Live fleet tracking
A live map shows every aircraft in your fleet, updated every few minutes. Distance from your airport, altitude, speed, and estimated arrival — so your team is always ready.
🚗
Crew vehicle management
Track fuel level, odometer, next service date, and condition for every crew car. Each vehicle has a QR code — scan it to update status in seconds, from anywhere on the ramp.
🔔
Push notifications that matter
Shift reminders, fuel alerts, aircraft arrival warnings, MX parts updates — delivered to every team member's phone automatically, no action required.
🛒
Shared supply management
One shared catalog of everything you buy, organized by store. Your buyer sees a cart grouped by store. Your mechanics get notified when parts are needed. No more duplicate orders or forgotten items.
📋
SOPs in their pocket
Step-by-step grooming procedures, vendor contacts, and shift handoff notes — always up to date, always accessible, even offline. New hires have everything they need from day one.

  • Automatic 60 nm arrival alert
  • ETA calculated from current speed and distance
  • No action required — runs automatically

Vehicle Management

Every crew car, always accounted for.

Each vehicle has a fuel gauge, odometer log, service tracker, and monthly photo audit. A QR code sticker on each car lets any ramp crew member update its status in seconds — no login, no navigation, just the form.

  • Fuel level, KM, and next service date per vehicle
  • QR code on each car for quick updates from the ramp
  • Monthly condition audit with photos
  • Push alert when fuel is low or service is due
